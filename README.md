## 🧭 Overview

This project presents a fully interactive **Microsoft Excel dashboard** built to analyze the sales and profit performance of Coca-Cola across the United States in 2022. The dashboard enables business stakeholders to quickly identify top-performing retailers, regions, cities, and beverage brands through visual charts, pivot tables, and slicers.

## 💡 Key Findings

```
✅  Walmart accounts for ~54% of total sales — dominant retail channel
✅  West region leads all regions in profitability ($790.52K)
✅  Coca-Cola brand has the highest operating margin at 39%
✅  New York is the #1 city by revenue ($391.8K)
✅  Sales peak mid-year, suggesting strong seasonal demand
✅  Average delivery days stand at 37 — logistics optimization opportunity
```
